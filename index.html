FAQ

Questions, answered

Where is my financial data stored?

Entirely in your own browser (IndexedDB). Statements are never uploaded. The only thing stored on a server is your email address — used solely for your account.

How does the AI run without sending my data anywhere?

The categorization and language models run on-device. They download once (opt-in) and then process your data locally — even offline.

Which banks and file types are supported?

Most major North American banks and cards via PDF statements, OFX/QFX, and CSV — with automatic format detection. Amex, RBC, TD, Scotiabank, BMO, CIBC, Chase, and more.

Do I need an account?

Yes — a free account is required to import and analyze your statements. It only stores your email; your financial data stays in your browser. The free plan covers your last 2 months of history; Pro unlocks unlimited history and premium features. You can also explore a sample-data tour without an account.

What does the free plan include?

Importing, built-in auto-categorization, the core dashboard and charts, and one account — for your most recent 2 months of transactions. Pro ($7/month or $70/year) unlocks unlimited history, AI chat, custom cards and rules, multiple accounts, family mode, and a referral program (give a month, get a month).

Can I try it before signing up?

Yes — choose “Take a tour with sample data” to explore every feature with dummy transactions, no account needed.

Does it work on my phone?

Yes. The whole dashboard is mobile-responsive — transactions become tap-friendly cards, charts resize, and the menu slides in. Add it to your home screen to use it like an app.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Export a full JSON backup anytime and restore it on another device.
